Getting Started

06 June 2020

Let The Journey Begin

Today marks the start of a journey for my daughter and me, to the world of the business of music. We’re starting from zero. This is our story.

Aisling is a smart, fun, energetic musical ball of an eighteen year old, a third of my own age.

We live in Ireland, in the world renowned historical city of Galway and a one minute walk from the cool salty waters of the Atlantic Ocean. Aisling has just finished her fifth year of the six year secondary school programme, while I mainly earn my keep as an ageing software developer.

In December 2019, Aisling began as a part-time worker in my startup side business helping local sports clubs. But three months later we were brought to a crushing halt, like so many others, by the coronavirus pandemic. Left flaying with our revenue stream completely dashed and uncertain about if or when it might return, we began looking for other options.

April 2020 we settled first upon the idea of doing local deliveries to people housebound during the Covid-19 lockdown. We set up our website, purchasing a wireless card reader, canvassed local shops, and even put our butcher’s price list online. Then we balked. We had foreseen a low upside (€2/delivery), but had completely overlooked the potentially huge downside should we acquire or transmit the pandemic virus. We cut our losses.

Back to the drawing board we went. Online sports training? No. Online piano lessons? No. Website development? No. We must have gone through twenty ideas or more, then: A jingle studio? No. Wait. Yes!

So that’s it. We are setting up a jingle studio.

We’re doing this, starting from zero. We know nothing about the business of music. We don’t even know what we don’t know. But we’re doing this none the less.

We’re starting on a journey, and you are welcome to join us. This is our story.
